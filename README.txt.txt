Welcome to Namaste Attendance System V1.0

This application (Web App) is QR biased.

has we are using two QR code.

First QR is for Site or working location. (Format : Site, Site ID, Work Order No, latitude,   longitude)
					   Example: Site, S002,    W0003,         13.0121728, 77.5815168 
Second QR is for Employee information. (Format : Emp, Employee ID, Employee Name).

In this QR stating words are key (Site & Emp).
